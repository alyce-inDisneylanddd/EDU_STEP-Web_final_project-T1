International Scholarships

SHE_CAN Scholarship

Overview

The SHE-CAN (Supporting Her Education Changes A Nation) Scholarship is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering young women from post-conflict and climate-challenged countries by providing access to U.S. college education, mentorship, and leadership training. The program aims to equip these women with the skills and support necessary to become leaders who can drive positive change in their home countries.

Scholarship Details

Eligibility Criteria

Women aged 17-20 from low-income households.

High school graduates or those graduating soon.

Residing within one hour of the capital city in their respective countries.

Demonstrated academic excellence and leadership skills.

Strong English proficiency.

Commitment to returning home and contributing to community development.

Financial Support

Funding for SHE-CAN scholars is provided through:

College-Funded Scholarships: Full-ride scholarships from U.S. colleges covering tuition, room, and board.

SHE-CAN Support: Coverage of additional costs not included in college scholarships, such as test preparation, visa fees, flights, textbooks, and health insurance.

Scholar and Family Contributions: Scholars are expected to cover personal expenses through on-campus employment, and families may need to fund travel to preparation classes.

Application Process:

The application process involves submitting academic transcripts, national exam scores, and information about family income. There is no application fee. The recruitment cycle typically begins in June or July, with various deadlines and interview processes following, depending on the country.
