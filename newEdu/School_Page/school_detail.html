Cambodia Academy of Digital Technology

Overview

The Cambodia Academy of Digital Technology (CADT) specializes in digital technology, offering programs in software engineering, AI, and data science. Focused on practical skills and industry-relevant knowledge, CADT prepares students for success in Cambodia’s growing tech sector, with experienced faculty and strong industry connections.
