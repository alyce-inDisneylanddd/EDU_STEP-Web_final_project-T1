National Scholarships

Techo Digital Talent Scholarship

Overview

The Techo Digital Talent Scholarship is a prestigious program designed to empower Cambodian students passionate about technology and innovation. This scholarship supports the country's vision for a thriving digital economy by nurturing talent in computer science, engineering, IT, and other tech-related fields.

Scholarship Details

For 2025 academic year

Number of Scholarships: This year 500 students are selected for the 2025 acedemic year.

Coverage: Full tuition fees and additional support for study materials (laptop) may be provided.

Eligibility: Open to Cambodian students with strong academic performance and a passion for technology. The scholarship is open to students who took the National Exam in 2024 only. Must got grade A , B and C.
