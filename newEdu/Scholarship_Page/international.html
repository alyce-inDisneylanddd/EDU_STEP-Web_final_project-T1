International Scholarship

Overview

The National Scholarship Program in Cambodia is an initiative by the Cambodian government aimed at providing financial support and educational opportunities to students pursuing higher education. The scholarship program primarily targets students with strong academic performance, those from low-income families, and individuals pursuing studies in priority fields such as science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).
